Blackjack

Game rules:
The aim of blackjack is to finish the game with a higher total than that of the other player, without exceeding 21.
Going over 21 is commonly known as ‘busting’ and means an automatic loss.
Please note there will be a total of 5 games and player 1 will start first.

START NEW GAME - Reset the field (meaning, scores go back to '0' and you'll begin a new match)
HIT - Take another card
STAY - stick with the cards you have and it's automatically the other players turn

Press 'START NEW GAME' to begin - Good luck & Happy playing!
